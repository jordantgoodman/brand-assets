Built for Comfort
Overview

The Pillar Nest is a dedicated lactation pod designed to provide privacy, comfort, and dignity for nursing parents. With ergonomic seating, hygienic surfaces, and thoughtful features like a swivel work surface and storage for supplies, it creates a calm and supportive space for pumping or breastfeeding.

Suitable for offices, universities, airports, warehouses, industrial facilities, and public environments, the Pillar Nest meets the highest standards of functionality and aesthetics while helping organizations meet compliance requirements.

Code + Compliance

Designed to meet FLSA, the PUMP Act, and the PWF Act — ensuring full compliance without costly renovations.
